A Uxn emulator for PalmOS

PalmOS is the operating system running on a wide range of handheld PDAs from the ~2000s. Many of the earlier devices ran a m68k CPU, and have fairly constrained RAM and processing power, but the keyboard/touch input present an appealing choice for a Varvara/Uxn VM host.

Status

The screen/mouse/controller/datetime devices are mostly functional (no start/select buttons, no way to input special characters via the keyboard). The file device is currently not implemented.

Performance is acceptable for non-realtime uses, though a little sluggish depending on how much of the screen is updated per frame.
